# Sheikh Al Kar Brand System

## Existing Logo Analysis

The current logo is a rounded square orange badge with a white interior arch, a stylized sheikh profile and keffiyeh, a bold Arabic wordmark, and a small Latin restaurant descriptor. The refresh preserves those recognition anchors while flattening the icon, tightening proportions, improving spacing, and replacing the orange palette with a more premium restaurant system.
